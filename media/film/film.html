My Kind of Film

Favorite TV Shows

Breaking Bad

A high school chemistry teacher turned methamphetamine manufacturer partners with a former student to secure his family's financial future.

Genre: Crime Drama

Creator: Vince Gilligan

Stars: Bryan Cranston, Aaron Paul etc.

Modern Family

The series follows the lives of three diverse but interrelated family set-ups living in suburban Los Angeles.

Genre: Sit-Com

Creators: Christopher Lloyd, Steven Levitan

Stars: Ty Burrell, Julie Bowen, Sofia Vergara etc.

Favorite Movies

Inception

Dom Cobb and Arthur are "extractors" who perform corporate espionage using experimental dream-sharing technology to infiltrate their targets' subconscious and extract information.

Genre: Sci-Fi Action

Director: Christopher Nolan

Stars: Leonardo DiCaprio, Joseph Gordon-Levitt

Interstellar

Cooper is enlisted to pilot the Endurance spacecraft through the wormhole as part of a mission to colonize a habitable planet with 5,000 frozen embryos and ensure humanity's survival.

Genre: Drama

Director: Christopher Nolan

Stars: Matthew McConaughey, Anne Hathaway
